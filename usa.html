UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

          The U.S. is a country of 50 states covering a vast swath of North America, with Alaska in the northwest and Hawaii extending the nation’s presence into the Pacific Ocean

        Tourism in the United States is a large industry that serves millions of international and domestic tourists yearly. Foreigners visit the U.S. to see natural wonders

5 BEST PLACES TO VISIT IN USA

1.Empire State Building: Climb 102 Floors

     (Where to stay..)

One of the most iconic things to do in the United States of America would be to visit the Empire State Building and channelize your inner athlete as you climb 102 floors of stairs! Even though it will be tiring, it will totally be worth the effort as you can catch a glimpse of the entire city of New York from there. And if you don’t feel like climbing on your visit, take the elevator that will show the view of the entire city with each passing floor.


Best time to visit:  April to October





Location:- 20 W 34th St, New York, NY 10001, United States


Entry Fee: INR 1424 + taxes


Timing: 8 AM to 2 AM




2.White House: Take A Guided Tour

     (Where to stay..)

This place has a very important role to play in making America what it is. All important political decisions are made in this place and you can take a guided tour here and know about the history of America. It is the official residence of the President of the States and you can take a look around at specific hours.


Best time to visit:  November to January and June to Augustr





Location:- 1600 Pennsylvania Ave NW, Washington, DC 20500, United States


Entry Fee: N/A


Timing: Tuesday – Thursday: 7:30 AM to 11:30 AM Friday – Saturday: 7:30 AM to 1:30 PM

3.Golden Gate Bridge: Catch A Glimpse Of The Sunset

     (Where to stay..)

One of the most iconic US landmarks and a constant backdrop in a lot of Hollywood films, the Golden Gate Bridge is a great place and visiting here is one of the top things to do in the USA for couples. Why couples specifically, you ask? Because if you’re crossing it during sunrise/ sunset, you’ll witness unforgettable views from here with your loved one and feel romantic vibes all around the place.


Best time to visit:  November to January and June to Augustr





Location:- Golden Gate Bridge, San Francisco, CA, United States


Entry Fee: N/A


Timing: 24-hours open

4.New York City

     (Where to stay..)

Iconic New York is a picturesque city where you can find splendid architectural tourist attractions. It is rated among the top places to visit in America in December due to its architecture and dining & shopping capital. Perhaps, this mind-blowing destination wears many crowns and is indeed an awesome tourist destination to be added to your list of places that can be visited in America in December. A city that never sleeps, is a cliche that epitomizes New York. With live music, karaoke, and dancing, New York should definitely be on your bucket list. Those who love hockey can even head to the Madison Square Garden where the ice hockey match, concert or basketball game are conducted.


Best time to visit:  November to February





Key attraction :   Brooklyn Flea Market, The Bryant Park, Central Park and Statue of Liberty


5.Los Angeles

     (Where to stay..)

Los Angeles is a center of the film and US television industry. Ideal place for all the movie buffs, there are several behind the scenes tours that are offered in Universal, Warner Brothers, and Paramount Pictures. Besides, major TV shows and movies being filmed in Los Angeles, there are attractive places also where you head to. One of the most famous cities worldwide, Los Angeles is known as the city of dreams and the city of Hollywood. A destination itself, Hollywood is positioned in the suburb of Los Angeles city. A glamour of silver screen, this tourist place is a must-see tourist attraction. Disneyland Park is a perfect holiday destination that is apt to indulge in various activities that are in coordination with the themes of Disneyland.


Best time to visit:  November to February





Key attraction :   everly Hills, Venice Beach, Santa Monica, and Walk of Fame Boulevard
